Indoor gardening is a fantastic way to bring a touch of nature into your home with minimal effort. Begin by selecting easy-to-care-for plants like snake plants, pothos, or spider plants, which thrive in low light and require infrequent watering. Choose pots with drainage holes and use a standard potting mix to ensure proper soil aeration. Place your plants near a window for natural light, or use a basic grow light if needed. Water your plants when the soil feels dry, and maintain a stable room temperature between 60-75Â°F. With just a bit of attention and care, you can enjoy the beauty and benefits of indoor plants, making your living space greener and more inviting.
